Services

Coaching built around real responsibility.

Every engagement is shaped by the client's actual responsibilities — not a generic curriculum. These are the four ways Firmitas works with leaders today.

The Flagship Engagement

One-to-One Executive Coaching

A six-month or longer engagement for founders, executives, and emerging leaders. Confidential, disciplined, and built around your actual responsibilities — decision-making, communication, team leadership, accountability, execution, conflict, personal discipline, calling, stewardship, and legacy.

For Small Leadership Teams

Small-Team Leadership Coaching

Individual and team coaching for approximately one to four leaders seeking clarity and alignment together — with clear sponsor expectations and confidentiality standards established up front, so every participant knows exactly how the engagement works.

Focused & Selective

Focused Leadership Workshops

Half-day or full-day sessions on topics already supported by the Firmitas methodology and the founder's experience. Selective by design — a workshop is offered only where it can be delivered with depth and conviction.

For Groups & Gatherings

Speaking & Leadership Teaching

Faith-centered executive leadership presentations for companies, churches, professional groups, and aligned organizations — practical teaching grounded in real executive experience and a biblical worldview.

How It Begins

Every engagement starts with a discovery conversation.

There is no pressure and no pitch. We talk about your context, your responsibilities, and what you want to build. If Firmitas is the right fit, we define the engagement together — scope, rhythm, confidentiality, and expectations — before anything begins. Investment is discussed openly in that conversation.
