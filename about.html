About Firmitas

A name that means what we do.

Firmitas is the Latin word for strength, steadfastness, and durability. In classical architecture it named the first requirement of any structure worth building: it must be able to stand.

The Name

Before anything can endure, it must be built to stand.

The Roman architect Vitruvius taught that every enduring structure requires three things: firmitas, utilitas, venustas — strength, usefulness, and beauty. Strength came first, because nothing else matters if the foundation fails.

We believe the same is true of leaders. Skills, strategy, and style all matter — but they rest on the foundation of character. Firmitas exists to help leaders build that foundation deliberately, so that everything built on top of it lasts.

Built on truth. Driven by purpose. Built to stand.

The Founder

Jonathan LaBoube

Jonathan LaBoube founded Firmitas after more than seventeen years of leadership experience across finance, operations, sales, strategy, and organizational growth. As a CPA, CFO, operator, and commercial leader, he has sat in the seats his clients sit in — carrying budgets, teams, targets, and the quiet weight that comes with all three.

That experience shaped a conviction: most leaders don't need more information. They need a confidential place to think clearly, a disciplined process for confronting what matters, and counsel anchored to something sturdier than the latest management trend. Firmitas combines practical executive experience with professional coaching discipline and a clear biblical worldview.
