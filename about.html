About Firmitas

Leadership is architecture.

Every decision, habit, relationship, and system either strengthens the structure or introduces hidden weakness. Firmitas exists to help leaders build on a foundation that can carry the responsibility they have been given.

The Name

Before anything can endure, it must be built to stand.

Firmitas is a Latin architectural principle associated with strength, stability, and durability. A structure can be beautiful and useful, but if it cannot stand, it has failed. Firmitas applies that same standard to leadership. Performance matters, but it must be supported by character, wisdom, disciplined execution, and faithful stewardship.

That architectural idea is the central metaphor of the brand: leaders are builders, character is structural, wisdom is foundational, and legacy is what remains after the builder is gone.

Built on truth. Driven by purpose. Built to stand.

The Founder

Jonathan LaBoube

Jonathan LaBoube founded Firmitas after more than seventeen years of leadership experience across finance, operations, sales, strategy, and organizational growth. His background as a CPA, CFO, operator, and commercial leader allows him to understand the real pressures executives carry. Firmitas combines that business experience with disciplined coaching and a clear biblical worldview.

His coaching philosophy is simple: the coach should not become the hero of the client's story. The goal is to help leaders develop the wisdom, self-awareness, courage, and discipline to lead well when the coach is no longer in the room.

Our Philosophy

Strength. Direction. Legacy.

Strength

Direction

Legacy
